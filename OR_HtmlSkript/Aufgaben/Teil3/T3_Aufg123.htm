 5 XI + 3 X2 + 4 X3 <= 100

 a) z = 5 X1 + 6 X2 + 7 X3

b) z = 5 X1 + 6 X2 + 4 X3

c) z = 5 X1 + 8 X2 + 6 X3 ?
