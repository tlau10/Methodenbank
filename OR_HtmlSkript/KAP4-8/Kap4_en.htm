4. Duality

4.1 A price problem

A company rents, based on the "machine hours", two production systems, which shall be used to manufacture two products. The rent shall be calculated in such a way that the total manufacturing costs for product 1 are at least 1 profit unit (PU), and for product 2 at least 2 profit units per rent unit (RU) - otherwise the provider would do it himself.

The machine capacities to be used are 3h/RU and 1h/RU for P1, and 2h/RU and 3h/RU for P2.

For the production itself, machine Ml is used 12 h, M2 9 h. Ascertain the rent calculation for 1h of Ml and M2, respectively !
